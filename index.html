Web audit projects

Analysis of factors affecting the performance of a website to identify areas for improvement. Once these aspects are identified, implementing enhancements to optimize design, web analytics, content, or site structure, among others.

Types of audits:

  • UX/CRO: Detailed analysis of websites to identify issues causing low conversion rates.
  • Web Analytics: Reviewing web data to ensure accuracy and reliability.
  • SEO: Detecting potential inefficiencies and areas of improvement, including technical aspects, content, and crawlability, to enhance organic search engine rankings.
  • Paid: Reviewing advertising accounts to identify potential areas for improvement.
  • ASO: App Store Optimization to ensure the app appears among the top results when users search in app stores.

CRO projects

Definition of strategies with the goal of enhancing the effectiveness of websites or applications to increase conversion rates.

These types of projects included as part of the methodology:

  • Analysis: Gathering and analyzing baseline data about user behavior, navigation, click patterns, etc., to identify problematic areas and improvement opportunities.
  • Hypotheses: Based on data analysis, hypotheses are formulated about how specific changes to the site can enhance the conversion rate.
  • Design and Testing: Proposed changes are implemented in the page design through A/B testing or other multivariate tests, comparing the proposed options.
  • Implementation: Once the variant that yields a higher conversion rate is determined, the changes are fully implemented on the site.
